How To Play

Players start as humans, with one randomly selected as the first zombie. Humans must explore, loot weapons, and escape using the train.

When infected, humans turn into zombies and hunt survivors.

Humans earn stars to unlock costumes and perks.

Zombie classes include Bomber, Catcher, Mimic, and Phantom, each with unique abilities.
