How To Play

Players start as humans, with one randomly selected as the first zombie. Humans must explore the map, loot weapons, and survive long enough to escape using the train.

When infected, humans turn into zombies and must hunt the remaining survivors. The zombie team grows stronger as more players get infected.

Humans can unlock costumes using stars earned from playing or winning matches. Some costumes provide useful perks.

Zombies can choose special types:

Bomber

Explodes and deals massive damage.

Catcher

High damage and balanced.

Mimic

Disguises as humans.

Phantom

Can turn invisible temporarily.

Humans win by escaping. Zombies win by infecting everyone.
